# Image Breed Dimensions Price Fun Fact
1 Poodle Standard is 22 inches tall,45-70 pounds $2000-$3500 The unique Poodle coat doesn’t shed the same way other dogs do. Because of this, they are considered hypoallergenic, or much easier to live with for allergy sufferers.
2 Yorkshire "Yorkie" Terrier Standard is around 10 inches tall,4-6 pounds $1200-$1600 These little dogs were once bred from a collection of terriers, including the waterside terrier, in order to hunt rats.
3 Maltese Standard is 7-12 inches tall,4-7 pounds $2000-$3500 The Maltese is one of the oldest-known breeds of dogs,and is said to be over 2800 years old.
4 Pitbull Standard is 18-21 inches tall, 30-60 pounds $900-$2000 Pit Bulls were considered to be so trustworthy with children that they were known as nursemaids or nanny dogs.
5 Labrador Retriever Standard is 22-24 inches tall,55-80 pounds $800-$1200 The Lab is built for swimming. This breed has a sleek, waterproof coat, webbed feet, and an otter-like tail.
6 Chihuaha Standard is 6-9 inches tall, up to 6 pounds $400-$1200 As of 2014, a Chihuahua named Milly - 3.8 inches tall (9.65 cm) - was officially recognized by the Guinness Book of World Records.
7 Rottweiler Standard is 24-27 inches tall, 50-105 pounds $2500-$4000 Rottweilers almost became extinct when the railroads came around, but where brought back when they became policedogs.
8 Beagle Standard is 13-15 inches tall, 22-30 pounds $800-$1500 The earliest dogs called Beagles were only 8 to 9 inches tall and could fit into the pocket of a hunter. They were referred to as Pocket Beagles
9 Bulldog Standard is 12-16 inches tall, up to 40-55 pounds $1500-$3000 Bulldogs love (or at least tolerate) being dressed up in clothes and costumes! So you can make them exude whatever personality you want, at least for a time.
10 Siberian Husky Standard is 21-24 inches tall, up to 35-60 pounds $1000-$1500 Known for being independent thinkers, Huskies will sometimes ignore your commands – choosing to see them more as thoughtful suggestions..
